🍺 Brewery Marketing & Business Strategy

Boston University
Sep 2025 – Dec 2025

Overview

Developed a comprehensive business and marketing strategy for launching a brewery in the Boston area. The project required market research, positioning analysis, operational planning, cost estimation, and promotional strategy design.

Problem

The brewery market in Boston is competitive, with many established local brands. A new brewery must clearly define its target customers, brand personality, competitive advantage, and marketing channels to attract customers and build loyalty.

My Role

  • Conducted internal and external business analysis.
  • Contributed to market positioning and customer segmentation.
  • Developed promotional campaign ideas aligned with sustainability and community engagement.
  • Helped estimate marketing and operational considerations.
  • Supported final presentation development.

Tools & Methods

  • Market research
  • SWOT analysis
  • Competitive analysis
  • Customer segmentation
  • Marketing strategy design
  • Business planning

Key Outcome

The final strategy positioned the brewery as a community-oriented and sustainability-conscious brand with targeted promotional campaigns designed to attract young professionals, local residents, and craft beer enthusiasts.

📦 Delivery Time Analysis for Food Delivery Platforms

Boston University
Sep 2025 – Dec 2025

Overview

Used SQL to analyze delivery time factors for food delivery platforms and identify operational insights that could improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Problem

Food delivery platforms must balance speed, service quality, driver availability, restaurant preparation time, and customer expectations. Delays can reduce customer satisfaction and damage platform loyalty.

My Role

  • Used SQL queries to explore delivery-related data.
  • Analyzed factors that may influence total delivery time.
  • Interpreted results from both technical and business perspectives.
  • Collaborated with teammates to generate recommendations.
  • Contributed to final reporting and presentation materials.

Tools & Methods

  • SQL
  • Data exploration
  • Data filtering and aggregation
  • Business interpretation
  • Team-based analysis

Key Outcome

The project produced practical recommendations for improving delivery operations, including identifying potential bottlenecks and connecting data findings to customer experience improvements.

🤖 Generative AI Food Waste Reduction App

National Taipei University
Mar 2025 – May 2025

Overview

Co-developed a mobile app prototype that uses generative AI to help users reduce food waste through personalized suggestions, recipe ideas, and better food management.

Problem

Food waste is a global sustainability issue. Many consumers waste food because they forget what ingredients they have, lack recipe ideas, or do not know how to use leftover items efficiently.

My Role

  • Co-developed the app concept and user experience.
  • Used generative AI concepts to design personalized food recommendation features.
  • Supported prototype development using CursorAI.
  • Designed a user-friendly interface to improve engagement and usability.
  • Connected sustainability goals with practical consumer behavior.

Tools & Methods

  • Generative AI
  • CursorAI
  • App prototyping
  • User experience design
  • Sustainability-focused innovation

Key Outcome

The prototype demonstrated how AI can be applied to everyday sustainability challenges by helping users make better use of ingredients and reduce avoidable food waste.
